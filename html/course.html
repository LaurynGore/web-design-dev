Description

BA (Hons) in Early Childhood Care and Education a course designed under imortant founations; theory, practice, research and reflective integration. The couse aims to create professionally qualified graduates who can work in a range of early years jobs, working with children up to the age of eight in both care and education settings.
The course includes the study of accompanying training of the disciplines of early years’ care, education, psychology, sociology, law and personal and professional development and supervised professional early years, practice placements. Note: Garda clearance is required for all students taking this course

Why This Course

Students will have the opportunity to complete various work-placements, in terms three, six and eight of the course. With this students will gain valuable skills in regions of care or instruction or both. These placement experiences will expand the students’ application of knowledge from the disciplines studied to professional practice. Students will conclude their fourth year with a written thesis which allows them focus on a specific area of interest to them in relation to early years’ care and education.
